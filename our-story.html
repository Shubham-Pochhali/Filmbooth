About Filmbooth.

Filmbooth began with a simple idea: Digital photos are easy to take, but they rarely feel special. We wanted to recreate the nostalgia of stepping into a vintage photobooth — the countdown, the shutter click, the printed strip, and the imperfect charm of analog photography — all inside a web browser.

LAUNCH BOOTH

Tactile Design

Every interaction was intentionally designed to feel tactile, from the camera interface to the printing animation and film-inspired layouts.

Privacy First

Unlike most online photo editors, Filmbooth processes everything locally in your browser. Your photos stay on your device.

Credits
